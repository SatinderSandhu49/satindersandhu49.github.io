Parents and Grandparents Super Visa Program allows parents and grandparents to come to Canada on a multi-entry visa that remains valid for up to 10 years. Unlike other visitor visas, a Super Visa allows visa holders to stay in Canada for up to two years on initial entry to Canada.

Requirements:

  1. A written commitment is required for financial support from their child or grandchild in Canada.
  2. Proof that shows that the sponsor in Canada meets minimum income requirements.
  3. Canadian medical insurance coverage of $100 000 for at least one year from the date of entry.
  4. Complete an immigration medical examination.
